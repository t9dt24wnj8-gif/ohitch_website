Electric Propulsion Video Archive

Electric propulsion hot-fire videos are surprisingly difficult to find, especially in-space footage, and many are at risk of being lost over time. This archive was created to preserve and bring them together in one place.

Enjoy the glow!

If you know of a thruster that is missing from this archive and there is a video of it, please submit it here.
